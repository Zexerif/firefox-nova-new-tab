Nova Minimal New Tab

Transform your new tab into a beautifully minimal, distraction-free dashboard inspired by the Firefox Nova design system. Nova New Tab is designed to give you everything you need.

Key Features:

Clean & Focused: A clutter-free interface featuring a simple clock, personalized greeting, and a subtle ambient background glow.

Interactive Companions: Choose an animated virtual pet (Black Cat, Fox, or Dog) to keep you company! They walk across the bottom of your screen, react to your cursor, and occasionally chase virtual mice or squirrels.(cause i wanted to add my cat)

Quick Search: A built-in search bar that routes queries directly to DuckDuckGo

Custom Shortcuts: Easily add, edit, and manage quick links to your most-visited websites.

Live Weather: Real-time local temperature and conditions to keep you prepared for the day.

 Highly Customizable: Toggle widgets on or off, switch temperature units, and choose between English, Spanish, French, and German localizations.

Privacy First: This extension is completely open-source, requires no accounts, and collects absolutely zero telemetry or personal data.
